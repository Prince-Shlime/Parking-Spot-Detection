The Process


        


        


          

            

              

                
              

              
FIND

              
We look for datasets or images of parking lots and annotate the data with bounding boxes.

              
The images are individually annotated so that accurate data can be fed to YOLOv5.

            

          


          

            

              

                
              

              
TRAIN

              
The data set is split into three sections: training, testing, and validation.

              
Each section has an important role in producing an accurate network.
